Overview of IIT Kharagpur

Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur) is a public research university established by the Government of India in Kharagpur, West Bengal, India. Established in 1951, the institute is the first of the IITs to be established and is recognised as an Institute of National Importance. In 2019 it was awarded the status of Institute of Eminence by the Government of India.[3]

IIT Kharagpur Admission Process

For BTech and BSc, the admission process is completely conducted by JoSAA after students appear for their JEE Main and JEE Advanced exam. For other courses, such as MTech, MSc, PhD a, students are required to appear for their specific entrance exams (national or university-level and attend exam-specific/ institute-based counselling process for seat allocation.

Step 1: STEP 1: IIT Kharagpur Application Process (All Courses)

The application process for a few popular courses completely online. Candidates seeking admission must apply via the following websites:

  • BTech and BSc/ BS aspirants must fill out the application form for JEE (Main) through the official website of the National Testing Agency (NTA) (nta.ac.in). The application fee for boys is INR 625 and INR 325 for girls. Read more
  • MTech candidates are required to register for GATE through the official website of GATE Online Application Processing System (GOAPS) (appsgate.iitd.ac.in). The application fee for boys is INR 1,500 and INR 750 for girls. Read more
  • MTech candidates are required to register for GATE through the official website of GATE Online Application Processing System (GOAPS) (appsgate.iitd.ac.in). The application fee for boys is INR 1,500 and INR 750 for girls. Read more
  • For MSc, candidates need to register for IIT JAM through the official website (iitr.ac.in). The application fee for others is INR 1,800 for other and INR 900 for female (all categories)/ SC/ ST/ PwD for one test paper. Read more

Step 2: Selection Rounds

After applying, the institute considers the merit of the candidates for MBA, LLM and PhD admission. For BTech, MSc, BSc and MTech courses, JEE Advanced, IIT JAM and GATE scores are considered for the selection of candidates. Additionally, admission to PhD and MS by Research is based on UGC NET/ CSIR NET and other national-level exam scores.

Step 3: Merit List/ Cutoff and Admission Confirmation

JoSAA, COAP and CCMN Counselling are conducted for seat allotment to BTech, MTech and MSc courses. A merit list is released by the institute for announcing the names of the candidates shortlisted for certificate, PhD and MS by Research courses followed by a counselling round. Candidates need to pay the admission fee to confirm their admission.

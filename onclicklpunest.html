    What is LPUNEST?

    LPUNEST is the acronym of Lovely Professional University National Entrance and Scholarship Test. It is conducted in online mode every year for admission to more than 150 programmes. The test acts as an Entrance Test as well as Scholarship Test. The amount of scholarship is determined by the marks and rank obtained by candidates in the exam. In the NIRF Ranking 2022 LPU appears in Top 100 in its most crucial Overall Category, which includes IITs and Central Universities. LPU is also ranked 51st in the list of all the government and private universities of the country.

    LPUNEST 2022 Application Process

    Candidates who are eligible for filling the application form can apply for the exam in online and offline mode. To submit the application form in online mode, candidates should visit admissions.lpu.in to complete the application process. On the other hand, candidates who want to apply offline should reach to the University campus and collect LPUNEST kit by paying the requisite fee. The last date to take admission for Phase IV was August 31, 2022.

    LPUNEST Prep Tips

    Candidates can follow LPUNEST preparation tips 2022 in order to obtain good marks in the examination. Some common tips that a candidate should follow while preparing for the exam are:

    ● Familiarity with the syllabus - Proper knowledge of syllabus keeps the preparation on track.

    ● Notes making - Candidates should make their notes as it is easy to memorize hand written notes.

    ● Regular revision - Candidates should revise their study material in regular interval of time so that they can retain the studied things while writing the exam.

    ● Solving previous years' questions - Solving previous years' questions help in getting an idea of the difficulty level, types of questions and analyzing the preparation level.

    ● Appearing in mock test - Take the mock test for time management so that no question left unattended in the exam.

    ● Time management - Time management is very important to attempt all the questions in the stipulated time and it can be achieved by practice.

    ● Good diet - Candidates should eat well in order to keep their mental and physical health at a good level. It is important because a healthy mind exists in a healthy body.

    ● Sleep better - Candidates should sleep at least 6 to 7 hours as this is the time when brain rests. Proper sleep is very important for memorizing and recalling the things.

    ● Regular exercise - Candidates may go for a walk or running to keep their mind fresh and active.



    LPUNEST 2022 Eligibility Criteria

    For admission to BTech Programmes at Lovely Professional University, candidates need to fulfil the following Eligibility Criteria.

    Nationality

    Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply for LPUNEST (BTech)

    Qualifying Examination

    Applicants appearing for LPUNEST (BTech) should have either completed or shall be appearing in 2021, in final examination of 10+2 from Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE, New Delhi) or The Council for Indian School Certificate Examination (CISCE, New Delhi) or State Board or any other Council, Institution, college etc. The board must be recognized by Council of Boards of School Education (COBSE), New Delhi or any other concerned apex body.

    Eligibility Criteria in the Qualifying Examination:

    ● Candidates must have passed the exam with 60%* aggregate marks in 10+2 or equivalent (with Physics, Mathematics and English) subject to qualifying LPUNEST (B.Tech.)

    ● There is 5% relaxation to North-East states and Sikkim candidates/ Defence Personnel and their Dependents/ Wards of Kashmiri Migrants as far as passing marks in qualifying exam are concerned.

    ● There 10% relaxation to outstanding Sports Persons (i.e. A/A+ category as per Sports Scholarship) as far as passing marks in qualifying exam are concerned, Subject to verification from Selection committee of the University. The selection committee must comprise Head (DSW), Director Sports, Deputy Director Sports, Concerned Coach and VC Nominee.

    Note: In case the applicant falls in both categories, then only one relaxation will be given.

    For B.Tech. Biotechnology (BT), B.Tech. (Food Technology), B.Tech. (Biomedical Engineering)

    Subjects considered: Physics, Chemistry, English & either Maths or Biology or Biotechnology; other things remaining same as above.

    For B.Tech. Chemical Engineering (CHE) and B.Tech. (Petroleum Engineering)

    Subjects considered: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and English; other things remaining same as above.

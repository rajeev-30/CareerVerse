IIT Kanpur Quick Update

IIT Kanpur is an autonomous engineering institute in Kalyanpur town of Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. IIT Kanpur is placed 5th in Overall, 6th for Research, and 4th in Engineering category of NIRF ranking 2022. IIT Kanpur admission 2022 for the B.Tech program has been concluded. The institute is currently inviting applications for admission to PhD/MTech/MS(By Research) Programs 2022-23. The last date to submit the application form is November 11. Additionally, IIT Kanpur is accepting applications for emasters degree program January 2023 batch. The last date to apply form the same is November 12. Also, applications for admission to the PGPEX VLM program 2023 batch are open till November 10. Candidates seeking admission at IIT Kanpur can apply online on the official website of the institute - iitk.ac.in. Furthermore, CEED 2023 registration has been extended till November 9. The exam will be held on January 22, 2023. The GATE 2023 exam will be conducted between February 4 and February 12, 2023.

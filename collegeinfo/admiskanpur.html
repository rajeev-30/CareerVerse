IIT Kanpur Admission Process

For BTech and BSc, the admission process is completely conducted by JoSAA after students appear for their JEE Main and JEE Advanced exam. For other courses, such as MTech, MSc, PhD and MDes, students are required to appear for their specific entrance exams and attend exam-specific counselling process for seat allocation.

STEP 1: IIT Kanpur Application Process 2022 (All Courses)

The application process for a few popular courses completely online. Candidates seeking admission must apply via the following websites:

  • BTech and BSc/ BS aspirants must fill out the application form for JEE (Main) through the official website of the National Testing Agency (NTA) (nta.ac.in). The application fee for boys is INR 625 and INR 325 for girls
  • MTech candidates are required to register for GATE through the official website of GATE Online Application Processing System (GOAPS) (appsgate.iitd.ac.in). The application fee for boys is INR 1,500 and INR 750 for girls
  • For MSc, candidates need to register for IIT JAM through the official website (iitr.ac.in). The application fee for others is INR 1,800 for other and INR 900 for female (all categories)/ SC/ ST/ PwD for one test paper
  • The aspirants of MDes need to apply for CEED before submitting an application form at the institute. The candidates also need to submit several supporting documents and an application fee during the application process. The application fee for boys is INR 3,200 and INR 1,600 for girls
  • For MBA and PhD, candidates are required to apply via the official website of IIT Kanpur.

Step 2: Selection Rounds

After applying, the institute considers the merit of the candidates for MBA and PhD admission. For BTech, MDes, MSc, BSc and MTech courses, JEE Advanced, IIT JAM CEED and GATE scores are considered for the selection of candidates. Additionally, admission to PhD and MS by Research is based on UGC NET/ CSIR NET and other national-level exam scores.

Step 3: Merit List/ Cutoff and Admission Confirmation

JoSAA, COAP and CCMN Counselling are conducted for seat allotment to BTech, MTech and MSc courses. A merit list is released by the institute for announcing the names of the candidates shortlisted for certificate, PhD and MS by Research courses followed by a counselling round. Candidates need to pay the admission fee to confirm their admission.

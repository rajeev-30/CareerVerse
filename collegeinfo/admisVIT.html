VIT Vellore Course Admission 2022: Eligibility, Selection & Fee

VIT Vellore conducts VITEEE for BTech admission, VITMEE and VITBEE for MTech and MBA admissions, respectively and VITREE for PhD admission. Also, the admission to a few other courses, including BSc, BSc (Hons), BBA, BA (JMC), BCA, BCom, MSc, MCA and MDes is based on the aggregate obtained by the candidates in previous qualifying degree/ exam (merit-based). However, the VIT University also accepts national/ state-level entrance exams for admission to a few of the remaining courses. VIT Vellore Admission Process 2022

VIT Vellore follows a multi-step admission process for all the courses offered by it. After the release of the applications, students are required to apply, and appear for the relevant entrance exam followed by other selection rounds. After that, final selection results are released by VIT after which interested candidates can pay the admission fee and confirm their admission. A step-by-step process is discussed below:

The common step-by-step VIT University application process is tabulated below:

Step 1.1: Visit the official website of VIT (vit.ac.in) to register and click on 'New Users may register here'.

Step 1.2: Fill in all the details and click on 'Register'.

Step 1.3: Enter the OTP sent to the registered mobile number and click on 'Verify OTP'.

Step 1.4: On the next page, click on 'Application Form'.

Step 1.5:Check the 'I am aware' box and click on 'I Agree'.

Step 1.6: Fill in all the other required details and click on 'Save'.

Step 1.7: Note down the application number and click on 'Proceed to Pay'.

Step 1.8: Choose the desired payment mode and click on 'Pay Now'.

Step 2: Slot booking for exam and selection process

Book slot for VIT-conducted entrance, appear for the exam and other selection rounds, such as PI.

Step 3: Declaration of results and fee payment for admission

After the conduct of the selection round and the entrance exam, final result will be released by VIT and interested students can pay the final admission fee to confirm their seats.

Overview of IIT Delhi

Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, popularly known as IIT Delhi, is a public research and engineering institute, established in 1961. The institute has its main campus located at Hauz Khas in Delhi, India. In addition, it has two extension campuses, one in Sonepat, and one in Jhajjar. It is one of the oldest IITs in India and has been declared as an “Institute of Eminence” by the government of India.

IIT Delhi is best known for its wide variety of engineering programs and outstanding placement records. The college has been consistently ranked #2 among the top engineering colleges in India . For the flagship B.Tech program, the institute has always been one of the top preferences among all IITs. The institute charges an annual fee of INR 8.3 Lakhs for the same. Candidates who have cleared JEE Advanced examination and have secured an all India rank (AIR) of 1-100 are more likely to get admission to the IIT Delhi B.Tech program.

Apart from the flagship program, IIT Delhi offers more than 100 courses under UG, PG, Dual, and Doctoral degrees. Additionally, the institute offers online certificate programs in the fields of Digital Marketing, Product Development, and Management. Check IIT Delhi Courses & Fees

IIT Delhi placement stats have significantly increased in the past years. During the 2021-22 placement drive, the institute witnessed a 45% increase in the number of placement offers. The highest salary package stood at INR 2 CPA (International) and INR 1.25 CPA (Domestic). Some of the top recruiters at IIT Delhi are Microsoft, Intel, Oracle, Tata Projects, EXL Services, ICICI Bank, Goldman Sachs, HCL, etc. IIT Delhi Placements

IIT Delhi Application Process 2022

For seeking admission to the courses offered at IIT Delhi, as a first step, candidates are required
to visit the official website of respective national-level entrance exam
conducting bodies and fill out the application form in an online mode. Further, candidates
are also required to fill IIT Delhi application form 2022 and pay an application fee of Rs 200 (Rs 50 for SC/ ST/ PwD) candidates). The steps to fill IIT Delhi application form 2022 are given below:

Step 1: Visit the official website of IIT Delhi (www.iitd.ac.in). On its homepage, click on ‘Admissions’. From the drop-down menu that appears, further, click on ‘Announcements’.

Step 2: The admission brochure, admission pamphlet and prospectus can be downloaded from this page.
Click on ‘Apply Now’ button to proceed further.

Step 3: The registration page opens up. Click on ‘New User Register Here’. Fill the registration form with
all the details, including name, email ID, contact number, course preference, etc. Also, create
an alpha-numeric password for future logins.

Step 4: Further, fill in the remaining application form by addressing other details,
including guardian info, address of communication, qualifications, etc.

Step 5: Upload all the required documents in PDF format. Also, upload a passport-size
photograph and signature and JPG/ JPEG format.

Step 6: Conclude the application process by paying an application fee using a debit card/ credit card/ net banking..

List of required documents:

Candidates need to submit scanned copies of the below-mentioned documents while filling
IIT Delhi application form in online mode:

  • Latest passport-size photograph
  • First and last pages of Aadhar Card/ Voter ID Card/ Passport
  • PAN Card
  • Admission offer letter
  • Entrance exam scorecard
  • Qualifying degree certificate
  • Qualifying exam mark sheet
  • Category certificate (only in case of candidates belonging to SC/ ST/ OBC/ EWS/ PwD category)
  • No objection certificate (only in case of part-time applicants)
  • Sponsorship certificate/ Relieving letter (in case of full-time sponsored/ full-time working candidates)
  • Medical certificate (in case of full-time candidates)

IIT Delhi - Indian Institute of Technology Scholarships and Funding

Scholarship 1

Institute Merit-cum-Means (MCM) Scholarships (Undergraduate Programs):

The Institute offers Merit-cum-Means scholarships to undergraduate students in engineering and technology.
These scholarships are offered to about 25% of the students. The present value of Merit-cum-Means scholarship is INR
1000/- per month for General/OBC/EWS students and the recipient is exempted from paying the tuition fee.

Scholarship 2

Scholarship provision for SC, ST & PD students (Undergraduate Programs):
Tuition fee exemption is admissible to all SC / ST/PD students irrespective of their parents’/ guardians’
income, Institute offers several other benefits to students from these categories.

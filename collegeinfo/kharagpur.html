IIT Kharagpur Quick Update

IIT Kharagpur is a public technical university in Kharagpur, India. IIT Kharagpur has concluded admissions for the B.Tech program. The institute is currently accepting application for MBA admission 2023 offered by VGSOM. The last date to apply for the same is January 31, 2023. Eligible candidates can apply online on the official website - som.iitkgp.ac.in. Further, IIT Kharagpur has closed registrations for PhD spring semester admission 2022-23. Written test and interview for shortlisted candidates will be held between November 14 and November 21, 2022. GATE 2023 application form correction window is open now, the last date to make corrections in the application form is November 11, 2022. The GATE 2023 exam will be conducted between February 4 and February 12, 2023.

Overview of IIT Kharagpur

Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur) is a public research university established by the Government of India in Kharagpur, West Bengal, India. Established in 1951, the institute is the first of the IITs to be established and is recognised as an Institute of National Importance. In 2019 it was awarded the status of Institute of Eminence by the Government of India.[3]

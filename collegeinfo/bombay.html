IIT Bombay Quick Updates

IIT Bombay has concluded admissions for the B.Tech program. The institute is currently conducting admissions for PhD (Spring Semester). Registrations for the same are closed now, the entrance exam will be conducted between November 20 and December 9. Furthermore, registrations for UCEED 2023 and CEED 2023 have been extended till November 4. The exam will be held on January 22, 2023. The GATE 2023 exam will be conducted between February 4 and February 12, 2023.

Overview of IIT Bombay

Established in 1958, IIT Bombay currently ranked 3rd in engineering colleges under latest NIRF 2022 rankings. IIT Bombay offers admission to all courses through various national level entrance examinations for e.g. admission in B.Tech is done through JEE Advanced, for M.Tech candidates required GATE score, M.Des through CEED, M.Sc through JAM.
IIT Bombay is famous for its flagship B.Tech Programs. Candidates with JEE Advanced ranks in top 2000 have chances to book their seats in IIT Bombay B.Tech Programs. Complete Fees for the B.Tech at IIT Bombay is INR 9.12 Lakhs.

IIT Madras Admission Process

For seeking admission to the courses offered at IIT Madras (except BSc Online, PhD and MS by Research), candidates need to visit the official website of respective national-level entrance exam conducting bodies and fill out the application form in online mode. IIT Madras admission process includes the application process, selection round, cutoff, merit list and counselling. The step-wise admission process is given below:

Step 1: IIT Madras Application Process

IIT Madras admission to UG & PG courses is based on national-level entrance exams, candidates need to visit the official website of various conducting bodies to fill out the application form and pay the required application fee online.

  • For BTech, candidates must fill out the application form for JEE (Main) through the official website of the National Testing Agency (NTA) (nta.ac.in). The application fee for boys is INR 625 and INR 325 for girls
  • For MTech, students must visit the official website of (appsgate.iitd.ac.in) to register online. The application fee for boys is INR 1,500 and INR 750 for girls.
  • For MSc, candidates need to register for IIT JAM through the official website (iitr.ac.in). The application fee for others is INR 1,800 for others and INR 900 for females (all categories)/ SC/ ST/ PwD for one test paper. Read more

For BSc and other courses, candidates can follow the steps given below:
Step 1.1: Visit the official website of IIT Madras (www.iitm.ac.in). On its homepage, click on 'Academics' and then on the ‘Study @IITM’ tab. Further, click on ‘Online BSc Programme’ from the drop-down menu.
Step 1.2: Read the admission guidelines carefully and further click on the ‘Apply Now’ button.
Step 1.3: Sign in using any Gmail account. Approve the declaration and hit the ‘Start’ button once done.
Step 1.4: Complete the application process by filling in other details, including personal information, address of communication, guardian details, and educational qualification.
Step 1.5: Conclude the application process by uploading all the necessary documents in PDF format along with a scanned passport-size photograph and signature in JPG/ JPEG/ PNG format.
Step 1.6: Pay an application fee of Rs 3,000 in online mode through SBI Collect using debit card/ credit card/ net banking.

Step 2: Selection Rounds

IIT Madras admits students to BTech courses on the basis ofJEE Main and JEE Advanced scores. Candidates are allotted seats in JoSAA Counselling for BTech, BTech-MTech (Dual Degree) and BS courses. Admission to MTech is through GATE followed by COAP Counselling. The institute considers IIT JAM and CCMN Counselling for MSc courses. Candidates can check with the admission office for the selection rounds of the rest of the courses.

Step 3: Release of Cutoff list, Counselling & Admission Confirmation

The cutoff for BTech, MTech and MSc courses is released by the respective counselling bodies. For merit-based courses, IIT Madras will release the course-wise merit list on its official website. As per the merit list, candidates are called for counselling for seat allotment. Shortlisted candidates and then required to pay the admission fee to confirm their seats.

IIT Madras Quick Update

IIT Madras is the best engineering institute among all the IITs in India. For the 6th consecutive year, IIT Madras is ranked at 1st in Engineering category of NIRF. IIT Madras has concluded admissions 2022 for the B.Tech program. The institute is currently inviting applications for admission to B.S in Data Science program January 2023 batch. The last date to apply for the same is January 15, 2023 and the exam will be held on February 26, 2023. Eligible candidates can apply online on the official website of the institute - iitm.ac.in. Additionally, IIT Madras is inviting application for PhD and M.S (Research) admission 2023. The last date to submit the application form has been extended to November 3. Furthermore, GATE 2023 application form correction window is open now, the last date to make corrections in the application form is November 11, 2022. The GATE 2023 exam will be conducted between February 4 and February 12, 2023.

Overview of IIT Madras

IIT Madras has 17 departments, 14 research centres, and a Continuing Education Programs Centre (CEP). All of the centres offer 121 courses in Engineering, Science, Humanities, and Management. IIT Madras has signed MOUs with a number of foreign universities for student exchange programs. As per the MoUs signed, students pursuing UG, PG, and Dual Degree programs at the institute spend a semester abroad for broad exposure. In addition, the institute also offers Joint Doctoral Programs (JDPs) in collaboration with 17 foreign universities. The institute also provides an AICTE-sponsored Quality Improvement Program (QIP).

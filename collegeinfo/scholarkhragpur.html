Scholarship 2

Merit-CumMeans (MCM) scholarship:
These scholarships are awarded from the Institute funds. The Institute shall award scholarships on the basis of merit-cum-means to all eligible students of the 4-year B.Tech.(Hons.), 5-year Dual Degree, 5-year B.Arch.(Hons.) and 5-year
Integrated M.Sc. courses.

    One of the fields of study that is becoming increasingly popular in India in Engineering. Almost all science students hope to be successful Engineers one day. Engineering is a field of study that uses science, technology, and math to create, develop, and maintain new machines, buildings, software, hardware, systems, and processes.

    The top Engineering colleges in India accept scores obtained by students in numerous national or state-level examinations, including JEE Main, TNEA, MHT CET, AP EAMCET, TS EAMCET, and many more. There are several BTech colleges in major cities across the country such as Delhi/ NCR, Chennai, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Pune, Mumbai, Kolkata, and so on.

    Engineering Colleges in India accepting JEE Main: Eligibility Criteria

    The eligibility criteria for the BTech courses may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria for BTech are as follows. The candidates:

    • Candidates must have earned a passing grade in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in the 10+2 exam from an accredited board.

    • Additionally, they must have earned a minimum total grade in the aforementioned disciplines of 60%.

    • For admissions, candidates must take standard entrance exams like JEE Main, TNEA, MHT CET, and other university-specific entrance exams in addition to their 10+2 results.

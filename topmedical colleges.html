    • Top Medical Colleges in India

    The success in the medical field depends significantly on the institution where students pursue their medical course. Therefore, all the students who are presently preparing for their class 12th board exams should start identifying the best medical colleges in India, which will help them plan their future course of action accordingly. Admission in private and government MBBS colleges in India is conducted on the basis of performance of the students in the NEET UG Exam.

    The total number of recognised medical colleges in India is estimated to be above 1,100; making it difficult for the students to identify the best suited medical college for their requirements. These top medical colleges differ in various key aspects such as infrastructure, specialisations, fee structure, eligibility criteria and several others. Some of the leading medical colleges in India are AIIMS, Christian Medical College, Maulana Azad Medical College etc. The average annual fee for medical colleges in India varies greatly, starting from Rs. 7,000 for government colleges and going up to Rs. 25 Lakhs for private colleges.

    For your convenience, here we have listed the top medical colleges in India based on key parameters like rating, placement, reviews and several more.

    How to select the best medical college in India?

    With numerous medical colleges in India to choose from, selecting the best medical college can be a tough decision for the students. Any mistake in choosing the right college can have serious implications on the future prospects for the students. Thus, it becomes essential for the students to undertake in-depth research before reaching a final decision. Here are some useful tips to help you select the best medical college in India: -

    - When you are shortlisting a college, it is important to check the specialisations offered by them. Not all medical colleges offer all specialisations, and then some colleges are well-known for certain specialisations. So, investing some time to check the various specialisations on offer is a wise decision.

    - Use the power of social media and connect with the current students and alumni of the college. This way, you will be able to get comprehensive details about various aspects of the college, helping you make a final decision.

    - The infrastructure of the college plays a key role in ensuring a superior learning experience. Undertake some research regarding the physical infrastructure of the college such as hostel facilities, library resources, classroom facilities etc.

    - Make sure that the college is accredited with UGC and the Medical Council of India.

    - If you like to enjoy sports or music or other extra-curricular activities, make sure that the college offers the facility.
